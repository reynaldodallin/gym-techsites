How It Works

From Idea to Published in Minutes

A streamlined 5-step process that handles assessment, planning, training, and tracking — so you can focus on results.

01

Sign Up Online

Start with Strength Training by entering a target workout, or use Group Classes by pasting any session URL. Each mode triggers a different research and fitness plan to match your goals.

  • Strength Training: AI researches the fitness market and identifies content gaps
  • Group Classes: AI analyzes the source and finds a unique angle
02

Get Your Assessment

Select the training program (Standard fitness, HIIT Circuit, Comparison, Yoga Flow, Spinning, or Bootcamp), choose your language, and set your target goals and timeline.

  • Body composition and flexibility testing
  • Free 7-day trial for all new members
  • Choose your preferred class time and book instantly
03

Get Your Custom Plan

Our dual-model AI engine (Unlimited group classes) researches the topic, analyzes competitor content, writes the session, and optimizes it for search — all in under 60 seconds.

  • Competitor fitness market analysis and content gap identification
  • AI-powered research, writing, and optimization
  • Real-time fitness score calculation during generation
04

Train With Confidence

View your generated content with a detailed fitness score breakdown. See workout usage, heading structure, readability metrics, and optimization suggestions — all in one clear view.

  • Detailed fitness score with factor-by-factor breakdown
  • Inline editing for quick adjustments
  • Workout density and placement visualization
05

Track Your Progress

One click to book your next class or personal training session. Check in at the front desk with the FitPower app and start training immediately.

  • Instant class booking and check-in
